Baklava

A crispy, flaky pastry filled with nuts and topped with a honey syrup!

Ingredients

  • For the Baklava
    • 8oz filo pastry
    • 6oz walnuts
    • 6oz pistachios
    • 2 tbsp sugar
    • 1 tsp cinnamon
    • 1 tsp ground cloves
    • 4oz butter
  • For the Syrup
    • 1/2 cup water
    • 1/2 cup sugar
    • 1/4 cup honey
    • 1 tbsp lemon juice
    • 1/2 orange zest
    • 3 cloves
