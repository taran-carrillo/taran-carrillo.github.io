Pacific Rhododendron

Pacific Rhododendron or Rhododendron macrophyllum

The Pacific Rhododendron is also known as the Coast Rhododendron, California Rhododendron, or California Rosebay.

It grows anywhere from 2–9 m (7–30 ft) tall — taller in the shade than in the sun.

Found west of the Cascades from southern British Columbia to Northern California.

It grows in fairly dry open forests and edges of roads and other open non-wetlands.
