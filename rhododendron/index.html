Pacific Rhododendron or Rhododendron macrophyllum The Pacific Rhododendron is also known as the Coast Rhododendron, California Rhododendron, or California Rosebay. It grows up anywhere from 2–9 m (7–30 ft) tall, it is taller in the shade than in the sun. The Pacific Rhododendron is found west of the Cascades from southern British Columbia to Northern California. It grows in in fairly dry open forests and edges of roads and other open non-wetlands.
